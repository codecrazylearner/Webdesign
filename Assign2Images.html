" Easy Spinach Patty Recipe"

Baked Spinach Patties

This recipe is a healthy vegetarian version patty using spinach mainly.😀 It has simple ingredients found in the pantry of any home and easy to make. 😊

I like this recipe as it is healthy and a customizable recipe to suit every dietary restrictions or needs.😄 Any ingredient in the recipe can be substituted for any other ingredients.😋

Ingredients

  • Cut Frozen Spinach 2 cups
  • Boiled Peanuts 1/4 cup
  • Carrots Shredded 1/4 cup
  • Onion diced 1/2 cup
  • Salt to taste
  • Eggs 2
  • Oil 3tbsp
  • Chilli Powder or any spice of your choice 1/2 tsp
  • All purpose filour 2 tbsp
  • Bread crumbs 3 tbsp

How to prepare

  1. Defrost the Spinach
  2. Squeeze all the water from the spinach F609;
  3. Add all the ingredients to a bowl except eggs
  4. Beat Eggs
    • Separate egg yolks from egg whites
    • Whip egg whites with a whisk until peaks are formed
    • Add the yolks to the bowl with all the ingredients
  5. Mix all the ingredients along with the yolks
  6. Add the whisked egg whites to the mix and fold in
  7. Form medium size patties of the same size
  8. Spray oil on the sheet and transfer them to a baking sheet
  9. Bake them at 360 degrees for 25 minutes
  10. Make sure to flip the patties halfway
  11. Enjoy with ketchup or any spicy sauce of your choice

NOTE:

I forgot to mention in How to prepate that you need to preheat the oven for 350 degrees for 10 minutes before preparing. 😧

You can also add any grains like quinoa, brownrice or nuts or vegetables which can be turned as burgers as it is heavy but in this recipe I want lighter patties 😄

The patties can get very dry and hard on the outside if it is baked in a higher temperature.😞The outside of the patties develops a caramelization and the inside will be soft.😐

Spinach taste will be more prominent as it is used in generous amounts. 😝The color of the patties are green but the amount of spinach can be reduced and replaced with meat,veggie,nuts or even grains.😎
